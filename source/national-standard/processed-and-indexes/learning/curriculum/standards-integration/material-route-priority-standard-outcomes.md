# Material Route Priority Standard Outcomes

Date: 2026-06-06

These are route-level priority outcome seeds for source-to-material planning. They do not replace full standards extraction.

| Subject | Domain | Route-level summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| science | scientific_inquiry | Family science materials should route through observation, question, simple investigation, evidence, and explanation. |
| maths | mathematical_reasoning | Math materials should route through concrete representation, comparison, explanation, and problem-story choice. |
| chinese | reading_expression | Chinese materials should route through listening, reading, retelling, expression, handwriting or creation according to age. |
| english | bilingual_input_output | English in generated materials must stay bilingual and follow the target child's recorded RAZ level. |
| computing | ai_era_competency | Computing and AI-era materials should route through safe parent-mediated tool use, evidence checking, privacy, creation, and reasoning. |
